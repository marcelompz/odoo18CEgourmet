Date :- 16/12/2025
Version :- 18.0.0.1
    ==> Improved the visibility logic of the Add Multiple Products button. The button is now hidden when invoices or vendor bills are created from Sales Orders
        or Purchase Orders, as well as when credit notes or refunds are created from existing invoices or vendor bills. It remains visible when invoices, vendor bills,
        credit notes, or refunds are created directly from Accounting or Invoicing.
